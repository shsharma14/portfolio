About

I am a graduate student at The School of Information Studies, Syracuse University majoring in Applied Data Science.

Currently I am working part time as a graduate faculty assistant for the course Introduction to Applied Data Science in R where my main responsibilities are to assist students with R programming and Data Science pipeline.

I started my career in 2017 with Citigroup, India as an Assistant Manager. Over the course of 3 years, I worked in two teams in different business units. My first team was a part of the commodities business unit where I was put in the user interface team. My main tasks were to enhance the application that helped traders analyze their profit and loss and allowed them to hedge trades. Apart from that, I wrote unit test cases and helped the team achieve their goal of increasing code coverage from around 20% to 40% in that year.

After completing one year, I got promoted to another team in the Settlements division where I worked on reducing manual work of users by writing efficient SQL queries, optimizing existing stored procedures and building new modules for an application that helped clients safekeep their assets with Citi.

Before joining Citi, I completed my undergraduate education from Indian Institute of Technology, Varanasi that is ranked in top 15 engineering institutions in India. My formative years there were crucial in deciding my career. I always wanted to move to the United States to pursue higher education. To fulfill that goal, I did two research internships at universities in South Korea and Belgium.
